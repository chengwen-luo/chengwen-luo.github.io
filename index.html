Short Bio

I obtained my Ph.D degree from School of Computing, National University of Singapore (NUS). Before joining Shenzhen University, I was a postdoctoral researcher at CSE, The University of New South Wales (UNSW). Currently I am a professor in National Engineering Laboratory for Big Data System Computing Technology, Shenzhen University.

My research aims at exploiting collaborative sensing to develop fundamental technologies that are essential in building innovative Internet of Things (IoT) systems. My current research area mainly focuses on mobile computing and Internet of Things.
